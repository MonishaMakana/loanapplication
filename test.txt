Loan Application Test Plan

Files:
- loan-app.xhtml
- style.css
- test.txt

Purpose:
This project now uses only three files and avoids .html, .js, .php extensions.

Manual Test Steps:
1. Open loan-app.xhtml in your browser.
2. Select a loan type and click Next.
3. Enter valid Personal Information: Full Name, DOB, Email, Phone, PAN, Aadhaar.
4. Enter valid Address details and verify City autocomplete suggestions.
5. Enter Loan Details and ensure conditional fields appear for Home and Business loans.
6. Enter Employment or Business details and verify business registration/GST fields only appear when required.
7. Upload income, identity, and address documents via drag-and-drop or click.
8. Draw a signature on the canvas and clear if needed.
9. Accept Terms and Data Consent, then submit.
10. Verify the summary and pre-approval section displays an approval badge.

Validation Checklist:
- Step navigation works across all 8 steps.
- Real-time validation error messages appear under invalid fields.
- PAN accepts values like AAABP1234A and rejects invalid formats.
- Aadhaar accepts values like 1234 5678 9012.
- Postal code validation requires 6 digits.
- Loan amount is limited between ₹50,000 and ₹50,00,000.
- Home loan property field appears only for Home loans.
- Business turnover field appears only for Business loans.
- Business registration and GST fields are only required for Business employment type.
- Documents accepted: PDF, JPG, PNG, max 5MB.
- Signature capture works and can be cleared.
- Auto-save indicator updates after 10 seconds and reload restores form values.
- Summary page shows loan type, personal details, pre-approval status, and uploaded document counts.

Notes:
- The application logic is embedded in loan-app.xhtml.
- style.css contains all styling.
- test.txt documents the current test plan and operation instructions.
